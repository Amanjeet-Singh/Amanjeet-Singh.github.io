AI and Machine Learning

A look into AI and Machine Learning in Web Development

Artificial Intelligence is a widely discussed area of technology these days. One of the most important areas in web development these days is machine learning, which is a critical part of the research in Artificial Intelligence.

What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning is a division of research in Artificial Intelligence, and it pertains to algorithms and code written that helps machines categorize things, and sometimes do certain tasks based on the results of the categorization. An oversimplified explanation would be that machines are given loads of datasets to analyze and categorize, and are coded in such a way that makes them better suited for categorizing more of these datasets by learning. Machine learning is heavily inspired by deep learning and neural networks.

There are three types of machine learning: supervised, unsupervised and reinforced. Supervised learning involves the researchers essentially telling the machine what it should look for when analyzing, or a basic outline of what each substance/dataset is. Unsupervised learning involves giving the machine not many instructions, but instead asking it to look for patterns in raw data. Reinforced learning involves a method of trial and error system of rewarding and penalizing (I have no idea how one would reward or penalize a machine) the machine to make it learn.
